About Us

Lab Crystals with its wealth of past experience, expert team and state of the art equipment aims at catering to needs of both public and private clients by providing high-quality geological sample preparation and analytical services for clients from industrial and academic sectors worldwide. We intend to advance as an industry leader through quality client services, unique capabilies, professional development, process innovation and client-driven growth strategies. Our biggest goal is to provide a comprehensive range of services and cater to all the geo-engineering needs esp. Preparation of samples for metallography & petrography needs of our clients.
